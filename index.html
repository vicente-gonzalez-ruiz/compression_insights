Data compression insights

Vicente González Ruiz

December 29, 2018
  • Lossless compressors are bijective functions which ﬁnd a diﬀerent output for each possible input. For this reason, text compressors are lossless by deﬁnition.

  • Lossy compressors are surjective functions and therefore, two or more inputs can produce the same output. There are lossless audio, image, and video compressors, but most of them are lossy (although some of them can be lossless if quantization is not used).
